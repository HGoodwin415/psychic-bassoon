Top three activities to do in Minnesota

The Mall of America, located in Bloomington, Minnesota, is the largest mall in the United States, featuring over 500 stores, numerous restaurants, and entertainment attractions including an indoor amusement park and aquarium. Opened in 1992, it attracts millions of visitors annually from around the world.

Gooseberry Falls, located along Minnesota's North Shore, is a stunning state park known for its beautiful waterfalls, scenic hiking trails, and picturesque views of Lake Superior. It's a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers seeking to explore the area's rugged beauty.

The Minnesota Zoo, located in Apple Valley, is a leading zoo known for its expansive exhibits featuring animals from diverse habitats around the world. Opened in 1978, it focuses on conservation, education, and providing interactive experiences for visitors of all ages.
